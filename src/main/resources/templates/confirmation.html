Office of the City Clerk Vital Records Division

Need to Know:

WHERE TO OBTAIN A BIRTH RECORD CENTRAL OFFICE
The Office of the City Clerk Vital Records Division maintains records of births, deaths, and fetal deaths that occur in the County of Bexar. Birth records are available from 1897, death records from 1873, and fetal death records from 1952.

ADDRESS
Vital Records Division, George Whitfield, Jr. Municipal Records Facility, 719 S. Santa Rosa, San Antonio, TX 78204

OFFICE HOURS
Monday, Tuesday, Thursday, Friday - 8:00 a.m. - 5:00 p.m. Wednesday - 8:00 a.m. - 5:30 p.m.
The Office is closed on approved City Holidays and Special Events.

PHONE NUMBER
210.207.8781
