SAN ANTONIO VITAL RECORDS

The San Antonio Division of Vital Records (San Antonio Vital Records) issues certified copies of San Antonio death certificates for events which occurred in Bexar County from 1873 to the present.

SAN ANTONIO DEATH CERTIFICATES

San Antonio Vital Records issues only certified copies of a death record that can typically be used to obtain death benefits, claim insurance proceeds, notify social security and other legal purposes.
