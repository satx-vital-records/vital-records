SAN ANTONIO VITAL RECORDS

The San Antonio Division of Vital Records (San Antonio Vital Records) issues certified copies of San Antonio birth certificates for events which occurred within San Antonio from 1895 to present. San Antonio Vital Records also issues birth certificates for events which occurred anywhere in the state of Texas from 1926 to present.

SAN ANTONIO BIRTH CERTIFICATES

San Antonio Vital Records issues only certified copies of San Antonio birth certificates, which are typically acceptable for passport, social security, employment and personal identification purposes.
